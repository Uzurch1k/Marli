Courier service for direct transports

No time for diversions? Operating as a courier service specializing in time-sensitive deliveries, we operate across Germany and various European nations daily. Our operations entail collecting shipments from airports, transporting goods to trade fairs, and facilitating inter-European transfers.

To facilitate this, we maintain a vast network of over 40,000 vehicles, ensuring swift and flexible availability within 60 to 120 minutes. Additionally, we offer the option of booking documented delivery and acknowledgment letters.

Our clientele primarily consists of industries such as mechanical engineering, event management, logistics, and electrical engineering, catering to both large corporations and medium-sized enterprises.

Our courier service adeptly handles diverse shipments, ranging from documents to pallets, ensuring prompt and direct delivery to recipients both nationally and internationally. Whether it's sending parcels or arranging pickups, our couriers excel in swift execution. Pricing quotes for our services are conveniently accessible online, with real-time tracking available through user accounts.
